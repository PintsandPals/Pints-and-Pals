08/03/26

👀 Last week, the party faced a Beholder and survived the impossible! Paul Tree turned fear into triumph, Garjuk defied a 350ft fall, Tia shattered the mind-controlling device, even as stone Ventus grappled the beast, and Keohe landed the final, earth-shaking blow. They uncovered a mysterious meteorite that sprouted a giant beanstalk into the sky… but secrets remain. Hidden tunnels, a strange statue, an invisible man, and Ventus’ return to flesh await. ⚔️ Join the next session for the next chapter in our 2026 campaign!

01/03/26

🌌 The party delved deeper into the meteorite, and the weirdness escalated. They discovered Mikula, a monk of the Poison Hand, who transformed Mugu’s deadly poison into a healing salve. Meanwhile, Sir Dav and the Coot unearthed yeti-hide armour and a mammoth tusk longbow in the lower caverns. Deeper still, they met the enigmatic Dr Brains, who warned of strange lights, eerie noises, and an invisible stalker. A foe who could turn the party invisible too! Chaos erupted, allies collided, and only Garjuk’s quick thinking saved the day with Faerie Fire and healing. The chase led them to a balcony overlooking the meteorite’s hollow core… and a Beholder patrolling below. The adventure intensifies. Don’t miss the next session of our 2026 campaign!
