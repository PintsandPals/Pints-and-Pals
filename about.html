About Us

Founded in May 2025, Pints & Pals is a TTRPG group based in London and Edinburgh who run inclusive weekly and monthly TTRPG events.

Vinnie

Vinnie is undoubtedly the most reckless and risk-chaser of the group. He will gladly take on the most dangerous tasks for himself, and his idea of a good time is facing the biggest threat to his life he can find.

Throttle

Throttle has a natural flair for command. He has a keen gasp of responsibility and the ability to come up with a plan for any situation. When the bros ride into battle, it's Throttle who decides on the strategy, assessing the situation with lightning-quick speed and immediately knowing what to do.

Modo

Modo is the most laid back and the gentlest of the Biker Mice; while he enjoys the thrills of fighting bad guys, his idea of a perfect day is a quiet fishing trip, especially with his nephew Rimfire.
